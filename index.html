L-Sit to HSPU

The Hybrid Training System

1. The "Newborn" RPE Compass

Check this every morning. Be honest with your energy levels.

Sleep Quality Fatigue Level The Adjustment
Good (6+ hrs) Feeling Fresh Push: Complete all sets/reps at max effort.
Broken (4-6 hrs) Caffeinated Maintain: Drop 1 set from every exercise.
Poor (< 4 hrs) Zombie Mode Minimum Dose: 5 mins HS holds + 2 L-sit holds. Done.

2. Mandatory 10-Min Warmup

Before every Workout A, Workout B, or Bouldering session.

  • Wrist Circles: 15 reps each way.
  • Finger Pulses: Tabletop position, lift palms while keeping fingers down (2 x 15).
  • Wall Slides: Arms in "W" to "Y" against wall, keep lower back flat (15 reps).
  • Scapular Shrugs: Plank position, sink/push chest without bending elbows (2 x 12).
  • Cat-Cow: 1 minute fluid movement.

4. The Phase Progression

Phase 1: The Foundation (Weeks 1–6)

Goal: Build structural integrity in shoulders and core.

Workout A (Pressing):
  • Pike Pushups (Floor): 3 x 8–10
  • Box Handstand Hold (Feet on chair): 3 x 30s
  • Dips (Bench/Bars): 3 x 12
Workout B (Core/Skill):
  • Tucked L-Sit (on chairs/blocks): 4 x Max Hold
  • Hollow Body Holds: 4 x 30s
  • Wall Walks: 5 reps (Walk up wall to HS, then down)

Phase 2: The Vertical Build (Weeks 7–12)

Goal: Move weight to the wall and straighten the legs.

Workout A (Pressing):
  • Decline Pike Pushups (Feet elevated): 4 x 8
  • Wall HSPU Negatives: 5 x 3 (5-second slow descent)
  • Planche Leans: 4 x 15s
Workout B (Core/Skill):
  • One-Leg L-Sit: 4 x 15s (per leg)
  • Wall-Free HS Practice: 10 mins (Kick up, pull feet away)
  • Shoulder Taps (In wall HS): 3 x 12 taps

Phase 3: The Integration (Weeks 13–20)

Goal: Transitioning from L-sit to the press.

Workout A (Pressing):
  • Deep Pike Pushups (Hands on blocks): 4 x 8
  • Full Wall HSPU: 4 x 5
  • Handstand-to-Headstand (Wall): 5 reps
Workout B (Core/Skill):
  • L-Sit to Tuck Planche: 5 attempts (Swing legs back)
  • Freestanding HS Practice: 15 mins
  • Seated Leg Raises (Compression): 3 x 15

5. Graduation Requirements

  • To move to Phase 2: Hold Tucked L-sit (25s), 10 clean Pike Pushups, Chest-to-Wall HS (45s).
  • To move to Phase 3: Hold Full L-sit (10s), 3 Full Wall HSPU, Balance "wall-free" HS (10s).
  • The Ultimate Goal: One clean rep: L-sit → Tuck Press → Handstand → HSPU → L-sit.

6. Post-Climb "Pre-Hab" (Saturday)

Essential to prevent "Climber's Elbow".

  • Wrist Extensions: 3 x 15 (Light weight/water bottle).
  • Hammer Rotations: 3 x 12 (Pronation/Supination).
  • Rubber Band Extensions: 3 x 20 (Open hand against resistance).
  • Pigeon & Pancake Stretch: 2 mins each (Hip mobility for L-sit).

7. The "Newborn" Lifestyle Hacks

  • The "Doorframe Rule": Every time you walk through the nursery door, do a 20-second Wall Handstand.
  • The "Pram" Shrug: When walking with the stroller, lock your arms and push the handle down to "shrug" your shoulders away from your ears.
  • Tummy Time Plank: When the baby is on the floor, do your Hollow Body Holds or Planks right next to them.
